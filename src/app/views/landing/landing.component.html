What is GeoGo?

GeoGo was born with the idea to bring together colleagues, students or just travel mates. We believe that each place is unique in a way if it is presented properly. That is why, our team is constantly introducing new features.

The app is based on solving challenges that will bring you to the next stage. Various types of activities are offered from scanning a QR code to recoding a video.
